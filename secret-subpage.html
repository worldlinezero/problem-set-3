Confidential Coverups: The Supressed Truth About the Living Clouds of Almidrangh

An exercise in worldbuilding

Originally conceived by Aidan Bennett

We know few things about these lesions on our ecosphere. We know that a kairomancer, Zendalekh, was the one to summon them to our fair and humble Almidrangh. We know they will destroy our world without further thought, and without further intervention from individual like us here at Grenin. We reached out to your world in the hopes that you could help us. Not even our best technomancers could understand the technology you have at your disposal. Please, find the kindness in your hearts to help us.

Without it, we are surely doomed to a long and cruel suffering.
